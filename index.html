SteadyWords is different. It’s built for people who are tired, stressed, and still trying their best. We meet you where you are, without pretending everything is fine.

Frequently asked questions

Is this a replacement for therapy?

No. SteadyWords isn’t therapy and doesn’t replace professional help. It’s a small, daily support tool for people dealing with stress, overwhelm, and low mood.

What if I forget to check in?

That’s okay. There are no streaks here. You can turn on gentle email or in-app reminders, but missing a day doesn’t “reset” anything. You can’t fail at this.

I hate cheesy affirmations. Will this annoy me?

Honestly, probably not. SteadyWords is written for people who are skeptical of typical self-help. The language is calm, grounded, and realistic.

Do I have to pay right away?

No. You can stay on the free plan as long as you like. The Pro plan is there if you want more depth and flexibility.

Can I cancel the Pro plan anytime?

Yes. You’ll be able to cancel in a couple of clicks, and you won’t be charged again.
