Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how SteadyWords (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use this website and sign up to receive emails.

1. Information we collect

Email address. If you join the SteadyWords beta or mailing list, we collect your email address so we can send you updates.

Analytics data. We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly tool that does not use cookies or track you across the web. It provides us with anonymous statistics such as page views, approximate location (country/city), and device type.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Send you emails about SteadyWords, including updates, early access, and occasional surveys.
  • Understand how people use the site so we can improve it.

3. How we store and protect your information

Email addresses are stored securely with our email provider (such as Buttondown or another similar service). We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-party services

SteadyWords currently uses:

  • Plausible Analytics for privacy-friendly traffic statistics.
  • Email provider (for example, Buttondown) to manage mailing lists and send emails.

These services may process data on our behalf in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Your choices

  • You can unsubscribe from emails at any time using the “unsubscribe” link in any message.
  • You can contact us to ask what information we hold about you or to request deletion.

6. Children

SteadyWords is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we’ll update the date at the top and, where appropriate, let you know by email.
